**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
The application is a single-page app with role-based access control:
1.  **Salesperson View**:
    *   **Dashboard**: A read-only view of their personal key performance indicators (KPIs), charts, and financial summaries.
    *   **Follow-ups**: A lead management hub for their assigned leads, featuring a multi-step sales pipeline.
    *   **Data**: A master data table for their own leads with full CRUD functionality.
2.  **Admin View**:
    *   **Overview**: A read-only dashboard showing company-wide aggregate KPIs and performance metrics.
    *   **Salespeople**: A comparison dashboard with charts and rankings to evaluate performance across all salespeople.
    *   **All Data**: A master data table of all leads across the company, with a column indicating the salesperson for each lead. Admins can modify all data.
3.  **Authentication**: Users must register and log in. Salesperson accounts are restricted to a specific email domain (), while admin accounts are whitelisted.
4.  **Data Management**: Admins have access to a data import function (from an XLS file). Lead data is segregated by salesperson.
